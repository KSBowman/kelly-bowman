Milestone One Purpose, Audience, and Goal Documentation

Purpose:

My personal website project's purpose is to display both my skill as a web developer. In addition, I hope to convey my personality that I apply to the user interface by keeping it a clean, simple, direct and yet function layout.

Audience:

My sites intended audience will be primarily potential employers in the immediate future. Going forward beyond that, I will use it to showcase who I am and a portfolio of my work to solicit both promotions as well as freelance work.

Goal:

My goal is that I enhance my leverage in the "sales process" of obtaining employment in the near future post-bootcamp and freelance work beyond that timetable. In addition, I will use my page as a portal for recruiters and potential professional contacts to initiate communication with me.

Persona:

Name: Vickie R. Newman

Age: 43

Idealized User: The user is an employer looking to fill a new vacancy in their web development team. Upon receiving the approval for a new slot on their team they have come across my profile on LinkedIn and followed the link to my personal website to find out more about me.

Devices: The user is highly proficient on all modalities listed and has the following items available:

  • The company provided Dell XPS desktop at the office,
  • a personal Dell XPS laptop while at home and working remote,
  • a Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra with unlimited mobile hotspot set up with a VPN,
  • and multi-factor authentification set up on each layer of required credentials.

Attitudes and Needs:

  • Loves:
    • Flexibility while retaining quality
    • High speed and performance in all things
    • Functionality beyond the minimum required
    • Control over his domain
    • Autonomy and self-discipline it requires
  • Hates:
    • Quitting before all options are exhausted
    • Broken code
    • Lack of research prior to requesting resources from the team
    • Lack of team effort by team members
  • Needs:
    • A junior developer who is willing to learn new languages
    • A candidate willing to work for an industry and location based competitive wage
    • A team player who is able to integrate into the company culture
    • Some one who is willing to work through as needed in exchange for work hour flexibility

Goals: Vickie is determined to find a candidate that not only has the required foundational knowledge to get started with the team but also demonstrates the capacity to grow into a senior team member as the company is growing. Vickie is also unwilling to compromise on the fit of the new candidate as far as their soft-skills and team integration are concerned. She must also attempt to find this candidate quickly as she is pulling the extra weight for the vacant slot until the new hire is onboarded.

User Story: As a professional user, I want to see a candidate's skill level and personality prior to my decision to expel time to contact them.
