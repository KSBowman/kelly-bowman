Milestone One Purpose, Audience, and Goal Documentation

Purpose

My personal website project's purpose is to display both my skill as a web developer. In addition, I hope to convey my personality that I apply to the user interface by keeping it a clean, simple, direct and yet function layout.

Audience

My sites intended audience will be primarily potential employers in the immediate future. Going forward beyond that, I will use it to showcase who I am and a portfolio of my work to solicit both promotions as well as freelance work.

Goal

My goal is that I enhance my leverage in the "sales process" of obtaining employment in the near future post-bootcamp and freelance work beyond that timetable. In addition, I will use my page as a portal for recruiters and potential professional contacts to initiate communication with me.
